# Background Video

Place your gameplay video here as `gameplay-background.mp4`.

## Requirements

- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended for browser compatibility)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher recommended
- Duration: Loop-friendly, ideally 30-60 seconds of gameplay footage
- Content: FrenzyFront gameplay showing units, combat, territory expansion

## Recording Tips

1. Record at a zoomed-out view to show overall gameplay
2. Choose an active moment with lots of unit movement
3. Make sure the video loops smoothly (start and end at similar states)
4. Compress the video to keep file size reasonable (aim for < 20MB)

The video will be displayed with a dark overlay filter on the main menu page.
